# ব্যক্তিগত পেনশন বীমা পলিসি - Personal Pension Insurance Policy

## Overview
Professional and working people naturally want assurance of a worry-free, comfortable, and peaceful life in retirement. When there is no certainty of regular income in old age, Pension Insurance Policy provides regular monthly income. This plan is designed keeping in mind the financial needs of retirement life. People from any profession can take this policy. The main attraction is financial security for family in case of premature death during working life, which is not possible through any other savings medium.

## Key Features
- **Dual Benefit**: Financial security for family in case of premature death + lifetime pension after retirement
- **10-Year Guarantee**: If policyholder dies within 10 years of pension start, remaining 10 years' pension guaranteed to nominee
- **Death Before Pension**: If policyholder dies before pension start date, nominee receives higher of:
  - All paid premiums (except first year) with 7% profit, OR
  - 15 times annual premium
- **Accidental Death**: With DIAB, 30 times annual premium paid to nominee
- **Commutation**: Can commute 50% or 100% of pension amount at maturity for lump sum
- **Loan Facility**: Policyholder can take loan against policy
- **Tax Benefits**: Premiums are tax-free, income tax rebate on paid premiums
- **Premium Payment**: Annual or Semi-annual only

## Benefits
1. **Lifetime Pension**: Monthly pension as long as policyholder lives after pension starts
2. **10-Year Guarantee**: Guaranteed 10 years of pension payments
3. **Death Protection**: Financial security for family in case of premature death
4. **Tax Benefits**: Tax-free premiums and death benefits
5. **Loan Facility**: Can take loan against policy
6. **Commutation Option**: Can take lump sum instead of monthly pension

## Example
If a 35-year-old person takes a pension policy to receive 5,000 Taka monthly pension after age 57:
- **Annual Premium**: 9,210 Taka (for 5,000 Taka monthly pension)
- **Total Premium Paid**: 2,02,620 Taka (from age 35 to 57)
- **Annual Pension**: 60,000 Taka (5,000 x 12 months)
- **10-Year Guarantee**: 6,00,000 Taka guaranteed
- **Lifetime Pension**: Continues as long as policyholder lives

## Commutation Options
- **50% Commutation**: Can take 50% as lump sum, receive 50% as monthly pension
- **100% Commutation**: Can take full amount as lump sum

## Eligibility
- **Profession**: Any profession (employed, business, self-employed)
- **Premium Payment**: Annual or Semi-annual
- **Pension Start**: Can choose pension start age
- **Minimum Guarantee**: 10 years guaranteed pension

## Target Customers
- Working professionals
- Business people
- Self-employed professionals
- Anyone planning for retirement
- Those seeking lifetime pension
