# তিন কিস্তি বীমা (Three Installment Insurance)

## Overview
This is a term insurance policy popularly known as "Three Installment Insurance" among policyholders. The policy term is divided into three parts, and 25%, 25%, and remaining 50% of sum assured along with bonus is paid at the end of each part.

## Key Features
- **Three Installments**: Sum assured paid in three installments during policy term
- **First Installment**: 25% of sum assured after 1/3 of term
- **Second Installment**: 25% of sum assured after 2/3 of term
- **Final Installment**: Remaining 50% with bonus at maturity
- **Death Benefit**: Full sum assured with bonus even if installments are paid
- **Policy Terms**: 12, 15, 18, 21, 24, or 30 years

## Benefits
1. **Multiple Financial Needs**: Can meet financial needs multiple times during term
2. **Full Death Benefit**: Even after receiving installments, full sum assured with bonus is paid on death
3. **Investment Opportunity**: Can invest received installments to pay premiums
4. **Capital Retention**: First installment remains as capital

## Additional Benefits (with DIAB/PDAB)
- **Accidental Death**: Double sum assured if death occurs within 90 days of accident
- **Partial Disability**: 50% of sum assured for loss of one limb/eye
- **Total Disability**: Full sum assured for loss of both limbs/eyes or one hand and one leg
- **Permanent Disability**: 10% of sum assured annually for 10 years, all future premiums waived

## Installment Schedule
- **After 1/3 of term**: 25% of sum assured
- **After 2/3 of term**: 25% of sum assured
- **At maturity**: Remaining 50% with bonus

## Eligibility
- **Policy Terms**: 12, 15, 18, 21, 24, or 30 years
- **Premium Payment**: Annual or Semi-annual
- **Optional**: Accidental death and disability benefit (DIAB/PDAB)

## Why It's Popular
- Policyholder can use money during lifetime for profitable investments
- Full sum assured paid even if installments are received
- Death benefit protection throughout term
- Investment flexibility
