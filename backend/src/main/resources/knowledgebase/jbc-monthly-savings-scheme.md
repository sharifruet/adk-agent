# জেবিসি মাসিক সঞ্চয়ী স্কীম (লাভসহ) - JBC Monthly Savings Scheme (With Profit) - Table No. 61

## Overview
This scheme is designed to provide life insurance benefits to low-income people including farmers, laborers, cooperatives, livestock farmers, artisans, garment workers, rickshaw pullers, and especially women. People can pay low monthly premiums to get insurance benefits and their families will get financial security in case of premature death.

## Key Features
- **Term**: Minimum 12 years, Maximum 16 years
- **Premium Payment**: Monthly installments
- **Minimum Monthly Deposit**: 100 Taka
- **Maximum Monthly Deposit**: 1,500 Taka
- **Advance Payment**: Can pay up to 12 months premium in advance
- **Entry Age**: Minimum 18 years, Maximum 48 years
- **Maturity Age**: Not more than 60 years
- **Medical Exam**: Not required
- **Age Proof**: Required with proposal form
- **Surrender & Loan**: Available after 2 years of premium payment
- **Tax Benefits**: Income tax rebate on paid premiums

## Benefits
- Low monthly premium payment
- No medical examination required
- Maturity benefit with profit
- Death benefit with accrued profit
- Tax benefits
- Surrender and loan facilities after 2 years

## Eligibility
- **Entry Age**: 18-48 years
- **Maturity Age**: Maximum 60 years
- **Term**: 12-16 years
- **Monthly Premium**: 100-1,500 Taka
- **Documentation**: Only one specified form required
- **Age Proof**: Required
