# বহু কিস্তি বীমা (লাভসহ) - Multiple Installment Insurance (With Profit)

## Overview
Multiple Installment Insurance can meet financial needs multiple times before maturity. By purchasing this policy, policyholders can also ensure financial security for their dependents.

## Key Features
- **Multiple Installments**: Paid every two years after first four years
- **Full Death Benefit**: Even after receiving installments, full sum assured with bonus is paid on death
- **Policy Terms**: 10, 15, or 20 years
- **Premium Payment**: Annual or Semi-annual
- **Minimum Sum Assured**: 60,000 Taka

## Installment Schedule

### 10 Year Term:
- After 4 years: 20% of sum assured
- After 6 years: 20% of sum assured
- After 8 years: 20% of sum assured
- At maturity: Remaining 40% with accrued bonus

### 15 Year Term:
- After 4 years: 15% of sum assured
- After 6 years: 15% of sum assured
- After 8 years: 15% of sum assured
- After 10 years: 15% of sum assured
- After 12 years: 15% of sum assured
- At maturity: Remaining 25% with accrued bonus

### 20 Year Term:
- After 4 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 6 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 8 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 10 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 12 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 14 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 16 years: 10% of sum assured
- After 18 years: 10% of sum assured
- At maturity: Remaining 20% with accrued bonus

## Benefits
- Multiple financial needs met during term
- Full death benefit protection
- Regular installments every two years
- Bonus on maturity
- Financial security for dependents

## Restrictions
- Cannot combine with Family Security Insurance or Family Pension Insurance
- Minimum sum assured: 60,000 Taka
- Premium payment: Annual or Semi-annual only

## Eligibility
- **Policy Terms**: 10, 15, or 20 years
- **Minimum Sum Assured**: 60,000 Taka
- **Premium Payment**: Annual or Semi-annual
