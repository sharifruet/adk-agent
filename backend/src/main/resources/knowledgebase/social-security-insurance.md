# সামাজিক নিরাপত্তা বীমা (লাভসহ) - Social Security Insurance (With Profit) - Table No. 63

## Overview
This scheme is designed to provide life insurance benefits to low-income to middle-class people including farmers, laborers, cooperatives, livestock farmers, artisans, garment workers, rickshaw pullers, and especially women. This is JBC's social responsibility initiative to ensure everyone can get insurance benefits, save for the future, and ensure family financial security in case of premature death.

## Key Features
- **Term**: Minimum 10 years, Maximum 15 years
- **Premium Payment**: Monthly installments
- **Minimum Monthly Deposit**: 100 Taka
- **Maximum Monthly Deposit**: 5,000 Taka
- **Advance Payment**: Can pay up to 12 months premium in advance
- **Entry Age**: Minimum 18 years, Maximum 45 years
- **Maturity Age**: Not more than 60 years
- **Medical Exam**: Not required
- **Age Proof**: Required with proposal form
- **Surrender & Loan**: Available after 2 years of premium payment
- **Tax Benefits**: Income tax rebate on paid premiums

## Benefits
- Affordable monthly premium
- No medical examination required
- Maturity benefit with profit
- Death benefit with accrued profit
- Tax benefits
- Surrender and loan facilities
- Social security for low to middle-income people
