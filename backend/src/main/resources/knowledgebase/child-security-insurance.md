# শিশু নিরাপত্তা বীমা (লাভসহ) - Child Security Insurance (With Profit) - Plan 09

## Overview
This policy is issued jointly on the lives of premium payer and child. Usually, the child's father is considered the premium payer. If father is not alive or ineligible, mother can be the premium payer (must be educated - secondary or equivalent, and have own income). No one else can be premium payer. Child's maturity age should be between 18-25 years. This policy can be taken for minimum 6 months old child with terms of 8-24 years. Minimum sum assured is 6,000 Taka. Medical examination is required for both child and premium payer.

## Key Features
- **Joint Policy**: On lives of premium payer and child
- **Premium Payer**: Usually father, or mother if father unavailable
- **Child Age**: Minimum 6 months old
- **Maturity Age**: 18-25 years
- **Policy Term**: 8-24 years
- **Minimum Sum Assured**: 6,000 Taka
- **Medical Exam**: Required for both child and premium payer
- **No Supplementary Benefits**: Cannot add supplementary benefits
- **Surrender Value**: Available, but no loan facility

## Benefits if Premium Payer Dies
If premium payer dies before maturity:
- **Premium Waiver**: All future premiums waived from death date to maturity
- **Annual Benefit**: 100 Taka per thousand sum assured annually from death to maturity (or until child's death if earlier)
- **Maturity Benefit**: Full sum assured with bonus at maturity

## Benefits if Both Survive
If both premium payer and child survive to maturity:
- **Maturity Benefit**: Full sum assured with accrued bonus

## Benefits if Child Dies Before Maturity
If child dies before maturity, sum assured paid to premium payer as per following schedule:

| Child's Age at Death | Payment |
|----------------------|---------|
| 1 year               | 10% of sum assured with bonus |
| 2 years              | 20% of sum assured with bonus |
| 3 years              | 30% of sum assured with bonus |
| 4 years              | 40% of sum assured with bonus |
| 5 years              | 50% of sum assured with bonus |
| 6 years              | 60% of sum assured with bonus |
| 7 years              | 70% of sum assured with bonus |
| 8 years              | 80% of sum assured with bonus |
| 9 years              | 90% of sum assured with bonus |
| 10 years and above   | 100% of sum assured with bonus |

## Premium Rates
Premium rates vary based on:
- Child's age (nearest birthday)
- Policy term
- Premium payer's age (nearest birthday)

Rates are available for premium payer ages: 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45, 50 years

## Eligibility
- **Child Age**: Minimum 6 months
- **Maturity Age**: 18-25 years
- **Policy Term**: 8-24 years
- **Minimum Sum Assured**: 6,000 Taka
- **Medical Exam**: Required for both
- **Premium Payer**: Father (or mother if father unavailable and mother is educated with own income)

## Documentation
- Two separate medical examination reports required (one for child, one for premium payer)
- Age proof required for both

## Target Customers
- Parents planning for children's future
- Those seeking child education/security
- Families wanting financial protection for children
