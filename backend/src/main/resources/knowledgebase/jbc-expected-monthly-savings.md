# জেবিসি প্রত্যাশিত মাসিক সঞ্চয়ী স্কিম (লাভ সহ) - JBC Expected Monthly Savings Scheme (With Profit) - Table No. 62

## Overview
This scheme is designed for low-income people including farmers, laborers, cooperatives, artisans, livestock farmers, garment workers, rickshaw pullers, and especially women. The special benefit is that 30% of sum assured is paid as installment after half of the term, allowing financial needs to be met before maturity.

## Key Features
- **Term**: Minimum 12 years, Maximum 16 years
- **Premium Payment**: Monthly installments
- **Minimum Monthly Deposit**: 100 Taka
- **Maximum Monthly Deposit**: 1,500 Taka
- **Advance Payment**: Can pay up to 12 months premium in advance
- **Entry Age**: Maximum 48 years (for 12-year term), Maximum 44 years (for 16-year term)
- **Maturity Age**: Not more than 60 years
- **Medical Exam**: Not required
- **Age Proof**: Required with proposal form
- **Early Installment**: 30% of sum assured after half term
- **Surrender & Loan**: Available after 2 years of premium payment
- **Tax Benefits**: Income tax rebate on paid premiums

## Installment Schedule
- **12 Year Term**: After 6 years of regular payment, 30% of sum assured is paid
- **16 Year Term**: After 8 years of regular payment, 30% of sum assured is paid
- **At Maturity**: Remaining sum assured with accrued profit

## Benefits
- Early installment (30% after half term)
- Low monthly premium
- No medical examination
- Maturity benefit with profit
- Death benefit with accrued profit
- Tax benefits
- Surrender and loan facilities

## Monthly Premium and Sum Assured

| Monthly Premium | Term (Years) | Sum Assured |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| 100 Taka       | 12           | 12,500      |
| 100 Taka       | 16           | 17,500      |

## Eligibility
- **Entry Age**: 
  - 12-year term: Maximum 48 years
  - 16-year term: Maximum 44 years
- **Maturity Age**: Maximum 60 years
- **Term**: 12 or 16 years
- **Monthly Premium**: 100-1,500 Taka
- **Documentation**: Only one specified form required

## Death Benefit
Even after receiving first installment, if policyholder dies during term, full sum assured with accrued profit is paid to nominee.
