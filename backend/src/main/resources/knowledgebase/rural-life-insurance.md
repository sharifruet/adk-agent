# গ্রামীণ জীবন বীমা (লাভসহ) - Rural Life Insurance (With Profit) - Plan 91

## Overview
Low-income people, farmers, laborers, cooperatives, livestock farmers, fishermen, artisans, weavers, and any person can benefit from this plan with minimum cost. The main objective of Rural Life Insurance is to provide financial security for working-class people and undoubtedly deliver insurance benefits to the general public in rural Bangladesh.

## Key Features
- **Premium Rate**: Comparatively very low compared to other policies
- **Maximum Sum Assured**: Up to 20,000 Taka
- **Entry Age**: Maximum 40 years (usually without medical exam)
- **Age Proof**: Required with proposal form
- **Premium Payment**: Annual, Semi-annual, or Quarterly
- **Grace Period**: After 2 years, if premium not paid on time, policy won't lapse. Extended Term Insurance provides 12 months coverage, and policyholder can revive by paying arrears with interest
- **Tax Benefits**: Income tax rebate on paid premiums
- **Urban Eligibility**: Low-income urban residents can also avail this plan
- **No Rebate**: No rebate on large amounts and payment methods
- **Quarterly Premium**: Additional 0.50 Taka per thousand for quarterly payment
- **Bonus**: Profit bonus is given

## Benefits
- Very low premium rates
- Affordable for rural and low-income people
- Usually no medical exam required
- Flexible premium payment (annual/semi-annual/quarterly)
- Extended grace period after 2 years
- Tax benefits
- Bonus on maturity
- Suitable for farmers (can pay after harvest)
