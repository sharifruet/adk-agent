# মেয়াদী সুবিধাযুক্ত পেনশন বীমা পলিসি (Term Benefit Pension Insurance Policy) - Table No. 60

## Overview
This policy is designed to provide financial security for elderly people in Bangladesh. The scheme "Term Benefit Pension Insurance" has been introduced to ensure a worry-free, comfortable, and peaceful retirement life.

## Target Customers
- Employees in various positions
- Business people
- Expatriates
- Other professionals
- Those who want to ensure family financial security and receive pension after maturity

## Key Features
- **Entry Age**: Minimum 20 years, Maximum 60 years
- **Premium Payment**: Semi-annual and Annual
- **Policy Term**: Minimum 5 years, Maximum 45 years
- **Sum Assured**: Minimum 50,000 Taka, Maximum according to policyholder's capacity
- **Pension Options**: Policyholder can choose guaranteed pension for 10, 15, or 20 years
- **Death Benefit Before Pension**: If policyholder dies before pension start date, nominee receives full sum assured with accrued bonus
- **Death Benefit After Pension**: If policyholder dies after pension starts, nominee receives remaining term pension
- **Pension Start Age**: 55 to 65 years
- **Surrender & Loan**: Available during policy term as per conditions
- **Tax Benefits**: Income tax rebate on paid premiums
- **No Rebate**: No rebate on sum assured and payment method
- **Option C**: Applicable for this policy
- **Supplementary Benefits**: Can be added
- **Tax-Free**: Death claim amount is completely tax-free

## Benefits
- Financial security for family
- Guaranteed pension after maturity
- Tax benefits on premiums
- Flexible pension terms (10/15/20 years)
- Death benefit protection
- Loan and surrender facilities

## Eligibility
- **Age**: 20-60 years (entry age)
- **Premium Payment**: Semi-annual or Annual
- **Term**: 5-45 years
- **Sum Assured**: Minimum 50,000 Taka

## Common Questions

**Q: When does pension start?**
A: Pension starts between ages 55-65 years as chosen by the policyholder.

**Q: What happens if I die before pension starts?**
A: Nominee receives full sum assured with accrued bonus.

**Q: What happens if I die after pension starts?**
A: Nominee receives remaining term pension payments.

**Q: Can I get loan or surrender?**
A: Yes, available during policy term as per conditions.
