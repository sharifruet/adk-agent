# প্রমিলা ডি.পি.এস স্কিম (লাভসহ) - Promila DPS Scheme (With Profit) - Table No. 64

## Overview
Although named "Promila DPS Scheme", both men and women can purchase this policy. This scheme is designed for women's economic, political, social, and administrative empowerment. The first pregnancy clause does not apply for female lives in this policy.

## Key Features
- **Premium Payment**: Monthly installments
- **Advance Payment**: Can pay up to 12 months premium in advance
- **Term**: 5-15 years (any term within this range)
- **Entry Age**: Minimum 18 years, Maximum 55 years
- **Maturity Age**: Not more than 60 years
- **Medical Exam**: Not required
- **Age Proof**: Required with proposal form
- **Minimum Monthly Deposit**: 100 Taka
- **Maximum Monthly Deposit**: 10,000 Taka
- **Surrender & Loan**: Available
- **Tax Benefits**: Income tax rebate on paid premiums
- **Option C**: Only applicable for this policy
- **No Rebate**: No rebate on large amounts and payment methods

## Benefits
- Available for both men and women
- First pregnancy clause not applicable for women
- No medical examination required
- Maturity benefit with profit
- Death benefit with accrued profit
- Tax benefits
- Surrender and loan facilities
- Flexible terms (5-15 years)

## Eligibility
- **Entry Age**: 18-55 years
- **Maturity Age**: Maximum 60 years
- **Term**: 5-15 years
- **Monthly Premium**: 100-10,000 Taka
- **Documentation**: Only one specified form required
- **Age Proof**: Required

## Special Features
- Designed for women's empowerment
- Both genders eligible
- No first pregnancy restriction for women
- Flexible premium and term options
